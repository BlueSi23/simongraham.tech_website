title: Creative Technologist Personal Site
---

- **Purpose**: Capture the core requirements and scope for the creative technologist portfolio so work can safely resume after any context reset.
- **Primary Outcomes**:
  - Home page with hero, services, featured experiments, latest articles, and experience snapshot.
  - Experiments grid and detail pages powered by Firestore + MDX.
  - Contact form with Firestore storage and SendGrid notifications.
  - Token-gated availability page embedding a Cal.com calendar.
- **Audience**: Prospective clients, collaborators, and studios exploring creative technology engagements.
- **Tone**: Calm, grounded, collaborative, with a focus on de-risking and systems thinking.
