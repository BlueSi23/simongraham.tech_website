---
description: Why the site exists and what problems it solves
---

- **Positioning**
  - Portfolio and thought-leadership hub for a creative technologist working across immersive, LBE, and emerging tech.
  - Emphasis on bridging creative, technical, and operational perspectives.

- **Problems Solved**
  - **Clarity of services**: Clear articulation of offerings (discovery, creative tech direction, TPM, R&D).
  - **Evidence of capability**: Experiments and articles that show thinking, not just finished case studies.
  - **Frictionless contact & scheduling**: Opinionated contact form plus private, token-gated availability.

- **User Experience Goals**
  - **Dark, cinematic aesthetic** inspired by the reference image with strong typography and generous spacing.
  - **Mobile-friendly** layouts that preserve hierarchy and breathing room.
  - **Fast navigation** with clear page structure (Home, Experiments, Articles, Experience, Contact, Availability).
